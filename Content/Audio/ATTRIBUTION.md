# Looped Audio Attribution

Gameplay SFX under `/Game/Audio` are the project's original placeholder WAV imports
(portal, melee_swoosh, melee_impact, player_hurt, enemy_hurt, ranged_shot, jump, button).

A 2026-07-09 Kenney.nl CC0 pack pass was **reverted** — Sahar hated those sounds.
Kenney staging leftovers may still exist under `Tools/audio_staging/` and
`Content/Audio/_import/` but are **not wired** into gameplay.

## Policy

- **No voice-over / character VO** — dialogue stays text-only.
- New Kenney cue paths (melee_windup, ranged_telegraph, hazard_*, enemy_dodge,
  player_death, lever, card_confirm) are unwired / silent.
